What We Treat

Treatable Cases

Our aligners can effectively treat an array of malocclusions, giving you the smile of your dreams.

Crowded Teeth

When there isn't enough space in your jaw for teeth to fit normally, creating overlapping or twisted teeth.

Spacing

Extra space between two or more teeth, which can cause food to get stuck and lead to gum problems.

Overbite

When the upper front teeth overlap significantly with the lower front teeth, wearing them down over time.

Underbite

When the lower front teeth are in front of the upper ones, causing strain on your jaw joints.

Crossbite

When some upper teeth sit inside the lower teeth rather than on the outside when biting.

Open Bite

When the upper and lower teeth don't touch when the mouth is closed, making chewing difficult.
